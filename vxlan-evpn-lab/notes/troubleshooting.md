
# Troubleshooting Guide

## OSPF
- `show ip ospf neighbor`
- `show ip route`

## BGP EVPN
- `show bgp l2vpn evpn summary`
- `show bgp l2vpn evpn`

## VXLAN NVE
- `show nve peers`
- `show nve interface`
- `show vxlan vni`

## Multicast
- `show ip pim neighbor`
- `show ip mroute`

## General
- `ping <loopback> source loopback0`
- `traceroute`
